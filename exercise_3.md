# Exercise 3: Tone Variation with the Same Task

**Objective: Control tone and style with explicit instructions.**

Choose one task (e.g., "Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview").

Write two prompts:

One for a friendly tone

One for a professional tone

Compare the outputs and note how the tone changes while the content stays consistent.

## My Answers

**FIrst prompt: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview. Use a friendly tone**

**Its response:**

Email Subject!

Interview Invitation – We'd Love to Meet You! 

Hi [Candidate’s Name],

I hope you're doing well!

We really enjoyed reviewing your application for the [Job Title] position at [Company Name], and we’d love to invite you to interview with us.

We were impressed by your background and would be excited to learn more about your experience, skills, and what you’re looking for next. The interview will take place on [Date] at [Time] via [Location/Platform].

Please let us know if this time works for you, or feel free to suggest another time that’s convenient.

Looking forward to speaking with you!

Best regards,

[Your Name]

[Your Job Title]

[Company Name]

[Contact Information]

**Second prompt: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview. Use a professional tone**

Email Subject

We look forward to your response.

Sincerely,

[Your Full Name]
